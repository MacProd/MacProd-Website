About

What is MacProd?
  1. A student community dedicated to music production and audio engineering.
  2. An educational support for student producers, offering tutorials on digital audio workstations (DAWs), sound engineering techniques and music composition.
  3. A safe environment in which anyone interested in music production can learn, share and create in a collaborative environment.
What do we do? Activities:
  1. General meetings
  2. Sample challenge meetings
  3. Campus events (tents, music promotion, LiveLab)
Goals:
  1. ProdFest
  2. Music festival showcasing McMaster’s musical talent, producers and DJs - stay tuned!
