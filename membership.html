Official Member Signup Please help us out and sign up through the msumcmaster.ca (it’s free and only takes a couple seconds) The more official members we get, the more funds we receive. This means better events and prizes (licenses, plug-ins, drum kits, etc.)
  1. Visit the MSU website and find our club in the Clubs Directory page by searching for “McMaster Music Production Society”, or click HERE
  2. Click “Join Now” and you will be directed to checking out with a club membership in your cart. Fill in a quantity of 1 and add to cart. Your cart should update with your free membership.
  3. Click “View Cart” and then fill out the form. Click “Confirm Order”, and you’re all done! Thanks for becoming a MacProd member.
